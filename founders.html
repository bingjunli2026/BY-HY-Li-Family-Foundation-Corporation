Our Founders

Bingjun and Haiyan Li founded the BJ & HY LI Family Foundation in 2007. The Foundation pursues philanthropy by promoting the activities of successful charitable organizations that directly help society.

Both Bingjun and Haiyan Li came from simple origins, being the first in each of their families to attend college and graduate with professional degrees. Throughout their careers they believed strongly in hard work, diligence, and discipline. Striving to make a positive impact, Bingjun and Haiyan created the BJ & HY Li Family Foundation to help and support charitable organizations.
